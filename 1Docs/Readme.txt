*** Story ***

After the Hunter survived a harrowing trip to the Forbidden Forest, he returned
home to connect with nature and prepare himself for any future events that may
transpire involving ghouls.

A good thing too, because shortly after, all Hell broke loose and the demons
invaded Earth. The Yurei managed to steal some souls out from beneath Hell's nose
and gave herself enough of strength, allowing her to leave the forest and begin
sieging the humans and demons alike, with priority given to the former. Her hate
for them rises well above all else, since they are responsible for her misery.

Now Flynn Taggart has to deal with not just demons, but also ghouls. They rise
from the depths of the abyss to hunt him down and feast on his flesh and soul.
Can he survive the nightmare journey and destroy the Icon of Sin before it's too
late?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
=======================
***** Description *****
=======================
This mod causes ghouls to spawn during gameplay for an extra challenge. They've
decent health, strengths and weaknesses that can be used to exploit Hell's forces,
or even the ghoul's own. Find out about the ghouls' stories by playing the mod!

The OPTIONS menu has tooltips for all items, thanks to UZDoom's new feature.
Don't forget, there's a keybind for disabling ghoul spawning altogether if they
run too much interference!

Individual ghouls can be toggled, but having them all enabled is recommended in 
order to unlock talents to use against them.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*** SHARED INTERACTIONS ***
It's worth noting that all ghouls have certain interactions that are different
from vanilla demon behavior.

1. Every ghoul remembers all enemies that attack them, and will retaliate in order
of who recently did the most damage.

2. Ghouls can target archviles (and enemies with NOTARGET). Can be disabled in
the options menu, or modified using the GHOUTARG.json file.

3. All Ghouls immediately change target to whoever damaged them last.

*** The Ghouls ***
==================
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	** Jitterskull **
Often known as a brawler, this massive skeletal head of a giant is a fierce but
predictable behemoth. Fearless and mindlessly bloodthirsty, he charges in skull
first in an effort to devour his prey. He is the tankiest of all ghouls, matching
a Baron of Hell with some additional protections.

He can move up and down some medium-sized cliffs, and will not try attacking if
he knows his path is blocked. Most of the time. Being undead does bring about 
some fortunate brain rot proving he's not all *that* smart.

-- Defensive --
* Unholy Ward
The Yurei has placed a protective warding upon Jitterskull that negates most
explosive damage, just like Cyberdemons.

* Regeneration
Whenever he's devouring something, he'll retreat away from his current target and
regain a portion of his health back. The moment he says "OM NOM NOM", he is ready
to attack again. Damage taken while chewing is increased, so make good use of the
few seconds to destroy him!

-- Offensive --
* Lunge (Jitter)
Charges Jitterskull forward with his mouth open. Can carve through weak enemies
and chew them up all at once. Also scoops up and munches any corpses on the 
ground, regardless of his health.

Plasma rifles and chainguns are excellent for dealing with him.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	** Sjas **
A floating ethereal head, this screamer moves through walls as effortlessly as
a ghost. Yet in his rage, he gives away his position with idle screaming, alerting
humans of his presence.

-- Defensive --
* Vanish
Upon taking enough damage, Sjas will fade away in a puff of smoke and teleport away.
He also does this after attacking.

* Reflective Screech (NYI)
Sjas emits a strong sound barrier that blocks/reflects attacks, rendering him
briefly invulnerable.

-- Offensive --
* Sonic Scream
Upon getting close to a victim, his scream deals a lot of damage in a radius.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	** Creeper **
The ghoul of stealth and tiny-ism, the Creeper excels at hiding in plain sight.
He's particularly effective when crawling along corpse-strewn floors, making him
unnoticeable except for the breathing. He can move through solid walls as well,
but one can hear his breathing to indicate he's close by.

-- Defensive --
Creeper retreats whenever taking damage or performs a Soul Scare. He also heals
with damage dealt, and a portion of his maximum health per enemy damaged by
Soul Scare.

-- Offensive --
* Curse Balls 
Creeper throws cursed balls at players who look directly at him. This more easily
gives away his position, at the cost of him throwing projectiles that deal tiny
damage, but quickly slows the speed of victims hit. He also throws them accurately
on an intercepting vector base on their velocity. For NPCs, Creeper always throws 
curse balls that home in on them too.

* Soul Scare 
Creeper jumps up with a supernatural screech that vibrates the soul of his victims,
heavily damaging their physical flesh. This bypasses most traditional armor.
Stronger foes are more tolerant of this warping damage. 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	** Choke **
The fastest ghoul in the game, dodging like crazy and only stopping to spit
projectiles or bite his foes. Difficult to see and hit.

--- Offensive ---
* Blood Ball Big
Spits up a big ball of cursed blood that deals massive contact damage and some
explosive.

* Blood Ball Small
Rapidly up-chucks a bunch of smaller blood balls in a shotgun spread, one at a
time.

* Nibble
When close to his target, rapidly performs fast bites that heal him.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	** Frostbite **
Capable of moving in the air and having low gravity, 

-- Defensive --
Defenses consist of a soul shield that absorbs all damage and slowly regenerates
after not taking damage for a short time. Armor penetrating shots cannot bypass
this but deal heavily increased damage. 

-- Offensive --
* Frozen Breath
Damages enemies with cold air, passing through all obstacles and bouncing off
level geometry. Can be interrupted with enough damage.

* Bite
A small lunge capable of skewering enemies to pieces that regenerates shields.
Unlike Jitterskull, he does not pick up corpses if his shield is full. 


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	** Clowny **
Uses illusions to confuse enemies and damages them by drifting into them.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	** Soul Harvester **
Uses souls to attack his foes.

	** Yurei **
The one responsible for so much death and suffering, justifying it for her own
means. One could be sympathetic towards her, if she hadn't decided ALL of humanity
was evil in her mind. What she could do to help humanity with such power...

The Yurei is mostly invulnerable. She doesn't have her full power, but touching
her while fully physical will result in instant death if not invulnerable. She
can't harm anyone if she takes damage though, forcing her to retreat. 

She will respond in kind with several elemental attacks where she can't be harmed
while channelling, but can be afterwards.
